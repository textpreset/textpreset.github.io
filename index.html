Features

Everything you need to type faster

Built for people who type the same things every single day.

One tap in any app

The TextPreset keyboard appears in WhatsApp, Gmail, Slack, LinkedIn, iMessage, Notion — anywhere you can type. Your full phrase library is always one tap away.

💡

Smart templates

Not just saved text — phrases that adapt. Fill in a name, pick a date, choose from a list, or nest one phrase inside another. A guided sheet walks you through every variable.

🏷

Color-coded tags

Filter hundreds of phrases in seconds. Pin critical phrases to the top. Drag to reorder, or auto-sort by usage. Your library, organized your way.

📦

75+ ready-made templates

8 professionally written packs — Customer Support, Sales, Email Signatures, Developer, Social Media, Meetings, HR, and Greetings. Import any pack in one tap.

☁

iCloud sync

Add a phrase on your iPhone. It's on your iPad before you put the phone down. Automatic, silent, instant — no setup required.

📊

Statistics dashboard

See exactly how much time you're saving: total insertions, characters saved, and estimated minutes recovered — based on real typing speed.

Share with your team

Send a phrase as a deep link — your teammate taps once to import. Export your full library as JSON. Perfect for onboarding or syncing standards across a team.

11 languages

Full localization for English, German, French, Spanish, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Polish, Portuguese, Arabic, and Simplified Chinese.
